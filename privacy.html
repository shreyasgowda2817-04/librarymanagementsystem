Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy outlines how LMS Pro handles your data with integrity and security.

At LMS Pro, we understand that your academic and personal data is sensitive. We are committed to protecting your privacy through advanced security protocols and transparent data practices. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to provide a better library experience.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized experience, we collect basic account information including your name, institutional email, and phone number. We also track borrowing history and AI Assistant interactions to improve resource recommendations and system performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to manage your library account, process book loans, and provide tailored AI assistance. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All AI interactions are processed through secure channels and are used to refine our Neural Assistant's ability to help you find research materials.

3. Data Security and Privacy Shield

LMS Pro employs industry-standard encryption and our proprietary "Privacy Shield" technology. This ensures that your chat history and personal logs are isolated per session. As per our security protocols, AI chat history is automatically purged upon logout to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Integrations

We may integrate with secure third-party services like Google AI Studio (for Gemini API) and payment processors. These partners only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their functions and are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time through the System Settings. You may also export your data in a machine-readable format for your personal records.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via system alerts or email. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
